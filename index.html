Digital Capital Group

Digital Capital Group is a New Zealand-based applied AI capital group.

It identifies structural opportunities where existing AI capability can materially improve the economics or defensibility of mid-market businesses.

DCG establishes standalone operating companies around those opportunities, provides capital and intellectual property, and governs them with institutional discipline.

How We Operate

DCG does not provide consulting or advisory services.

It forms companies.

Each company is built around:

  • A clearly defined structural inefficiency
  • Proven, existing AI capability
  • A disciplined economic model
  • Independent operational leadership

DCG supplies capital, shared intellectual property, and governance oversight.

The objective is durable operating businesses, not experiments.
