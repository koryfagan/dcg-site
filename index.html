About

Digital Capital Group (DCG) builds institutional-grade AI products for New Zealand mid-market companies (20–500 FTE).

Our products embed structured decision architecture directly into core workflows — governance, risk, capital allocation, and operational control at the execution layer.

Each product is developed under controlled validation and tested against real commercial environments prior to release, with defined human control thresholds embedded in critical decision pathways.

The objective is durable operating leverage and decision integrity.

Founder

DCG is founded by Kory Fagan.

His work centres on embedding decision structure into operating environments — so governance holds under scrutiny, risk is legible, and critical choices remain defensible when reviewed later.
